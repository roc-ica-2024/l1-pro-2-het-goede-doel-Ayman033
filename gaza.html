Stop the genocide in Gaza

About Us

The war in Gaza has killed more than 14,000 children since October 2023. No place in Gaza is safe for children. They live in fear every moment of the day. Fear of violence, fear of losing loved ones and fear for their future. For a year now. Children in the West Bank are also affected.

In the Gaza Strip, almost 9 out of 10 people are displaced, and many of them have had to flee several times. They stay in overcrowded places without enough basic facilities such as shelter, clean water, and food. Aid from organizations such as UNICEF is only possible to a limited extent. The war also affects children in the West Bank, threatening their mental and physical well-being.

The Challenges

Gaza faces significant challenges, including genocide, poverty, and lack of access to basic resources. Many children are unable to attend school or receive adequate healthcare, putting their life at risk.
